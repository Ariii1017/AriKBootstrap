About

About Me

Hi, my name is Jui Ping, and you can also call me Ariel. I am a graphic design student at George Brown College in Toronto, originally from Taiwan — a small and beautiful island country.

I am passionate about creating meaningful visual communication, exploring how design can be simple, expressive, and accessible. I enjoy photography, cooking, and trying new things that broaden my creative thinking.

My goal is to continue growing as a designer while staying curious and excited about the world.
